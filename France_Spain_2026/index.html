Trip Design Principles

  • 1–2 anchor activities per day, max. Everything else is flexible.
  • Walking-based exploration. No packed sightseeing schedules.
  • Outdoor dining preferred. Terrace seating, casual, baby-friendly.
  • Flexible timing. Lucy sets the pace. Build margin for naps and weather.
  • Easy exits. Don't commit to things that are hard to leave with an infant.
  • Recovery before travel days. Light schedule the day before a long drive or train.
  • This is an operational document, not a dream board. Structured, practical, calm.
