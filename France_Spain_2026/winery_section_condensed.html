Top Two Winery Experiences

  • Couvent des Jacobins — Walkable, free private tour (Airbnb partner), confirmed baby-friendly. Pair with a village stroll. Best for Monday, May 18.
  • Château La Dominique — 7-min drive, modern estate, scenic rooftop lunch. Skip tour/tasting and just dine. Best for Sunday, May 17.

Other Notable Wineries

  • Troplong Mondot — Elite, Michelin restaurant, formal, less baby-friendly.
  • Villemaurine — High quality, walkable, tastings only, more formal.
  • Bernateau — Mid-tier, 10-min drive, small family estate, very relaxed.
  • Coutet — Intimate, garden, 20-min walk, pond with ducks.
